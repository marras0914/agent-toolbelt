Agent Toolbelt provides programmatic access to AI-powered analysis tools, including stock research tools (investment thesis, earnings analysis, insider trade interpretation, valuation snapshots, bear-vs-bull cases, comparison, and moat analysis), as well as general developer utilities (schema generation, text extraction, token counting, regex builders, and others).
